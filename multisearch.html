MultiSearch

Paste any amount of text into the center panel, then run one or more simultaneous searches. Each search gets its own highlight color. Click the color dot next to any search box to customize it.

Search: Type a term and press Enter or click the search icon.
Multi-line paste: Pasting multiple lines into a search box automatically creates one search per line.
Accents: Searches ignore accents, so "cafe" matches "cafe" and vice versa.
Visibility: Toggle the eye icon to show or hide a search's highlights.
Navigate: Click any result, use the Prev/Next buttons, or click a minimap marker to jump to it.
Minimap: Click anywhere on the right strip to scroll there. Hover a marker to preview the result.

Handles large texts efficiently. Everything runs entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent anywhere and no tracking of any kind is used. This page can be saved and run locally without an internet connection. The code and page may be used freely without any restrictions.
